Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2 Warzone is set in the fictional city of Verdansk, a sprawling urban environment with various points of interest, including dense urban areas, open fields, and industrial zones. Game Modes

Gameplay Overview

In Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2 Warzone, players are dropped into Verdansk via an airplane and must scavenge for weapons, armor, and equipment. The objective is to be the last player or team standing. Players must navigate the shrinking safe zone, avoid or engage enemies, and strategically manage resources. The game includes a unique feature called the Gulag, where players who are eliminated can fight for a chance to re-enter the game. The game also includes various in-game contracts that players can complete for rewards, such as money, loadout drops, and more.
