About

Counter-Strike was originally a mod for Half-Life, created by Minh "Gooseman" Le and Jess "Cliffe" Cliffe. It quickly gained popularity and was officially released by Valve Corporation in 2000. Counter-Strike: Global Offensive (CS:GO) is the fourth game in the series, released in 2012. Both games are known for their tactical gameplay, where two teams, Terrorists and Counter-Terrorists, compete to complete objectives such as bomb planting or hostage rescue.

Game Modes

Gameplay Overview

In Counter-Strike, two teams compete in rounds to complete objectives or eliminate the opposing team. Each player starts with a standard loadout and can purchase weapons and equipment at the beginning of each round using in-game currency earned from their performance. The game requires strategic planning, precise aiming, and teamwork to succeed. CS:GO introduced new maps, weapons, and gameplay mechanics while maintaining the core elements that made the series famous.

Installation Guide

Follow these steps to download and install Counter-Strike 2 and GO on your Windows PC:
